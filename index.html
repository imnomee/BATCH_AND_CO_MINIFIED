Zero landfill ethos

With a commitment to zero landfill practices and partnerships with ethical suppliers like Caravan Coffee Roasters, Batch & Co stands at the forefront of a sustainable coffee movement. Join us in our quest for exceptional taste with a conscience.

Award-winning, sustainable

As winners of Time Out awards for best coffee and recognized for our commitment to inclusivity, Batch & Co offers more than just a cup of joe. Step into our welcoming space and experience the difference community and sustainability can make in your coffee enjoyment.

Experience Excellence

Beyond coffee, Batch & Co delights in offering a variety of artisanal treats sourced from local producers. Our commitment to ethical sourcing extends to every bite, ensuring you savor not only the flavor but the impact of your choices.

From Farm to Cup

Dive deep into the world of coffee with Batch & Co as your guide. From the farms where beans are grown to our sustainable practices in-store, discover how every step of our process aligns with our values of ethics, quality, and sustainability.
